Omodewu Olanrewaju Eyitayo

Omodewu Olanrewaju Eyitayo (Lanre Omodewu) is a multi-disciplinary professional specializing in Data & Business Analysis, Brand Strategy, Creative Design, and Fashion Consulting. He combines analytical thinking with creative excellence to build brands, systems, and experiences that are both visually compelling and strategically sound.

With a strong foundation in data analysis, Lanre leverages insights to drive decision-making, optimize performance, and uncover opportunities for growth. As a brand strategist, he develops clear positioning, identity systems, and marketing approaches that help businesses stand out in competitive markets.

Beyond strategy, Lanre is a skilled creative designer known for crafting impactful visuals, brand identities, and digital experiences. His expertise also extends into the fashion industry, where he works as a fashion consultant and clothing brand designer helping individuals and businesses translate ideas into market-ready fashion concepts.

His work sits at the intersection of creativity, strategy, and data, allowing him to deliver solutions that are not only aesthetically strong but also results-driven.

Lanre is passionate about building innovative brands, empowering businesses, and creating meaningful value through design, analytics, and strategic thinking.
